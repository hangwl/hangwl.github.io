## Discussion Guide

### Opening (5 min)
- Thank them for their time
- Explain purpose of interview
- Confirm time available
- Ask permission to take notes

### Questions

#### Current State (10-15 min)

1. Can you describe your role and key responsibilities?
   - **Response:** [Notes]

2. Walk me through a typical day/week in your role.
   - **Response:** [Notes]

3. What systems/tools do you currently use?
   - **Response:** [Notes]

#### Pain Points & Challenges (10-15 min)

4. What are the biggest challenges you face in [area of focus]?
   - **Response:** [Notes]

5. What tasks consume the most time that you wish were faster?
   - **Response:** [Notes]

6. Where do you see inefficiencies in current processes?
   - **Response:** [Notes]

#### Future State (10-15 min)

7. What would an ideal solution look like for you?
   - **Response:** [Notes]

8. What would success look like? How would you measure it?
   - **Response:** [Notes]

9. What concerns do you have about potential changes?
   - **Response:** [Notes]

#### Wrap-up (5 min)

10. Is there anything else you think we should know?
    - **Response:** [Notes]

11. Who else should we speak with?
    - **Response:** [Names/roles suggested]
