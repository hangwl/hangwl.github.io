## Facilitation Tips

### Managing Discussion
- Use parking lot for off-topic items
- Ensure equal participation
- Timeboxing is essential

### Handling Challenges

| Challenge | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| Dominant participant | "Let's hear from others..." |
| Silent participants | Direct questions, smaller groups |
| Off-topic discussion | "Let's add that to parking lot" |
| Running over time | "We have X min left, let's prioritize" |
| Conflict | "Both perspectives are valid, let's..." |
