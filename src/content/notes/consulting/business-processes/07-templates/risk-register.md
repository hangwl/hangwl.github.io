## Risk Categories

- **Technical:** Technology, integration, performance
- **Data:** Quality, availability, access
- **Resource:** Skills, availability, turnover
- **Schedule:** Dependencies, estimation, delays
- **Scope:** Changes, clarity, creep
- **Client:** Stakeholders, decisions, changes
- **Commercial:** Budget, pricing, collection
- **External:** Regulatory, market, vendor

---

## Response Strategies

| Strategy | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Avoid** | Eliminate the risk by changing approach |
| **Mitigate** | Reduce probability or impact |
| **Transfer** | Shift risk to third party |
| **Accept** | Acknowledge and monitor |
| **Escalate** | Elevate to higher authority |
