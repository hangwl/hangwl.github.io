## Issue Categories

- **Technical:** System errors, bugs, integration failures
- **Data:** Data quality, access, availability issues
- **Resource:** Team availability, skill gaps
- **Process:** Process breakdowns, communication issues
- **Client:** Client-side blockers, decision delays
- **Vendor:** Third-party or vendor issues
- **Environment:** Infrastructure, access issues

## Escalation Criteria

Issues should be escalated to Steering Committee when:
- Critical priority and unresolved >24 hours
- High priority and unresolved >1 week
- Resolution requires executive decision
- Issue has significant budget/timeline impact
- Resolution blocked by external factors
