# Data and AI Engagement Documentation

Welcome to the documentation repository for **a consulting firm's Data and AI Team** engagement processes.

## Purpose

This documentation serves as a comprehensive guide for associates and consultants to understand the business processes involved in Data and AI consulting engagements.
