## Phase Deliverables Summary

| Phase | Key Deliverables |
|-------|------------------|
| **1. Opportunity** | Qualified Lead, Independence Clearance |
| **2. Proposal** | Proposal Deck, SOW, Signed Contract |
| **3. Initiation** | Project Charter, Project Plan, RAID Log |
| **4. Execution** | Working Code/Model, Status Reports, UAT Sign-off |
| **5. Closure** | Final Handover, Lessons Learned, Closure Report |
